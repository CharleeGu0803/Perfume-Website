My Passion

I am a graduate student with a major in computing, but my interests span far beyond my field of study. Among these diverse interests, perfume holds a special place in my heart.

My journey into the world of fragrances began five years ago, during a period when I was struggling with poor sleep. Serendipitously, I watched a video by a YouTube vlogger who shared her experiences with perfumes, including how a specific scent served as her "tranquility scent". Intrigued, I purchased the same perfume. To my delight, not only did it smell divine, but it also had a soothing effect on my mind. I made a ritual of spritzing it on my wrists before bed, a practice that greatly improved my sleep quality.

This initial encounter sparked a deep fascination with perfumes. I embarked on a journey of exploration, learning about the vast array of scents available. I began to pair perfumes with my outfits, selecting them based on the season to evoke either a sense of cool refreshment or warm comfort.

My passion for perfume has grown over the years, and I feel compelled to share this love with others. Unfortunately, most perfume-related websites today are primarily focused on sales, offering little educational content for those looking to deepen their understanding of fragrances. Seeing this gap, I envision creating a vibrant and engaging website dedicated to the world of perfume. This platform would not only introduce the rich history and diverse ingredients behind perfumes but also highlight the artistry of perfume bottles from various brands. My goal is to craft a site that is accessible, entertaining, and informative, catering especially to those new to the world of perfume, helping them to navigate this fascinating world without the pressure of immediate purchase.
